Wildscapes is a family-owned xeriscaping business, passionate about turning high-maintenance, water-intensive yards into gorgeous natural landscapes that are better for the planet and better for the pocketbook.

Why invest in a wildscape?

Water wise

In dry climates, up to 60% of all residential water is used for landscaping and lawns. Xeriscaping will cut your outdoor water use (and bill!) by 50-75%.

Low maintenance

Our landscape designs use native plants that will thrive naturally, with minimal care. No more mowing, no more chemical fertilizers and pesticides.

Pollinator friendly

Our native plants create a pollinator paradise, attracting the bees, butterflies, and birds that are so essential to a healthy ecosystem (not to mention fun to watch!).

Gorgeous

Xeriscaping is an eco-friendly choice that saves time and money, but it's also beautiful! Once your garden is in full bloom, bursting with color and life, you'll never look back.
